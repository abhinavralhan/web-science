Web Science



What is web science
Web science is an emerging interdisciplinary field concerned with the study of large-scale socio technical systems, particularly the World Wide Web. It considers the
relationship between people and technology, the ways that society and technology co-constitute one another and the impact of this co-constitution on broader society.
Web Science combines research from disciplines as diverse as sociology, computer science, economics, and mathematics.
Research groups
There are numerous academic research groups engaged in Web Science research, many of which are members of WSTNet, the Web Science Trust Network of research labs.
Health Web Science emerged as a sub-discipline of Web Science that studies the role of the Web's impact on human's health outcomes and how to further utilize the Web to improve health outcomes.
